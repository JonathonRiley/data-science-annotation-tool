About the Tool and its Creator


Produced by Jonathon Riley, PhD. Data scientist, with experience in recommendation systems and NLP, currently employed at Depop.

This is a free tool designed for data scientists, by a data scientist. After struggling to find a unified tool for labelling and annotating datasets for machine learning tasks and only finding different tools for each new task (unless I wanted to pay AWS), I decided to build my own. Happy annotating/labelling.

Comments always welcome, especially regarding bugs!
